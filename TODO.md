## üí° Implementation Tips

- **Start Small**: Pick 2-3 features from Phase 1
- **Test Often**: Show her each new feature as you add it
- **Keep It Simple**: Don't overcomplicate the UI
- **Mobile First**: Ensure everything works on phone
- **Personal Touch**: Customize messages and content for her
- **Surprise Factor**: Add features without telling her, let her discover
- **Regular Updates**: Add something new every week/month
- **Backup Data**: Always keep backups of photos and content
